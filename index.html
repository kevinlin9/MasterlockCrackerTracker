Masterlock Cracker Tracker

About
This webpage's purpose is to keep track of the combination numbers you have tried in the process of cracking a master padlock (Model 2002 or 2010). Hopefully this will make the process easier to deal with.
Instructions
Enter the number that you are testing as the first number. You will then enter T or F depending on whether or not the second number test worked or not. If the lock does not click after turning the dial clockwise and counter-clockwise, type F. If the lock clicks then type T. You will be prompted to enter the number the dial clicked on and then be shown the combination numbers of your lock.
